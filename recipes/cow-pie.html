Cow Pie

Desperate Dan's Delicious Cow Pie

If you love the taste of warm meat in your mouth and in your tummy.
Then come, and try desperate Dan's delicious cow pie.

Ingredients

  • Cow
  • Gravy(cow juice)
  • Pastry

Method

  1. Rustle down a cow
  2. Dispatch of said cow in a manner of your choosing
  3. Dice up the cow into manly bite sized chunks
  4. Get a good ol fire going
  5. Empty your bed pan to use as a pot
  6. Put the pot on the fire
  7. Put as much cow and cow juice in the pot as you can
  8. Leave it to cook for the day whilst you go find a woman
  9. Get woman to make you some pastry with flour and what not
  10. Put the pastry on the pot
  11. Enjoy!
