Festival Toilet - Cocktail Recipe

If you're already really drunk and feeling adventerous!

Picture the scene,you've just home from the pub or club.
You should probably go to bed, but instead you search your fridge and cupboards for more alcohol.
This is the hour of the Festival Toilet.

Ingreidents

  • Baileys
  • Cola drink
  • Vodka or Gin or Polish Chemicals

Method

  1. Take a glass or mug(preferably clean but not essential)
  2. Pour in the Baileys and the clear spirit for the kick
  3. Now add the cola drink, this will give the colour, curdled and frothy look of a warm well used festival toilet.
